Cinnamon rolls - are sweet, spiral-shaped pastries made from a yeast-leavened dough. They are flavored with a filling of cinnamon, sugar, and often butter, and are typically glazed with icing or cream cheese frosting.

Creamy pasta - is a rich and indulgent dish featuring pasta tossed in a smooth, velvety sauce made from cream or a combination of cream, butter, and cheese. Popular variations include Alfredo or carbonara.My friends love when im doing pasta.

Beshbarmak - is a traditional dish.It consists of lamb beef or horse, served over wide, flat noodles. The dish is typically seasoned with onions. Beshbarmak is a symbol of hospitality and is commonly served at special gatherings and celebrations.

Pelmeny - are traditional Russian dumplings made from thin, unleavened dough filled with minced meat, usually a mix of pork, beef, or lamb. The filling is often seasoned with onions, garlic, and black pepper.

Lagman - is a traditional Central Asian dish.It features hand-pulled noodles served with a flavorful stir-fry of meat, vegetables like bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, and carrots, and a savory broth.

Vermicelli with gravy - is a simple and hearty dish popular in Russian cuisine. It consists of boiled vermicelli, typically made from wheat flour, and a sauce made from meat or vegetable broth.

Advantages of cooking at home

Healthier Choices

Cooking at home allows you to control the ingredients and portion sizes, enabling you to make healthier meal choices. You can use fresh, whole ingredients and limit added sugars, sodium, and unhealthy fats, leading to a more balanced diet.

Cost-Effective

Preparing meals at home is often more economical than dining out or ordering takeout. Buying ingredients in bulk and cooking larger portions can save money, and you can repurpose leftovers for future meals, reducing food waste.

Creativity

Cooking at home provides an opportunity to experiment with different flavors and cuisines. You can customize recipes to suit your tastes or dietary needs, allowing for greater creativity and satisfaction in your meals.
